Reverse Flash

I'm Eobard Thwane.

About me:

In 2466, I inherited wealth and aimed to become the Flash. I bought the Cosmic Treadmill, hoping for super speed, but it scarred me instead. I used my wealth to look like Barry Allen. My time travel attempt with the treadmill went awry, placing me after Barry's death. Discovering posters labeling me "Eobard Thawne" and my evil deeds drove me insane, turning me into a psychotic villain.

Worst Crimes

  • Super Speed
    Eobard Thawne possesses superhuman speed, allowing him to move at incredible velocities. He can run on water, create whirlwinds, and vibrate through solid objects.
  • Time Travel
    Thawne is a skilled time traveler, often using his speed to manipulate time. He can travel to the past and future, which is a significant aspect of his character's abilities and storyline.
  • Molecular Manipulation
    He can vibrate his molecules to phase through solid matter and create powerful shockwaves or energy blasts when needed.
  • Genius-Level Intellect
    Eobard Thawne is exceptionally intelligent and has a deep knowledge of science, particularly in the field of speed force physics. He is a strategic thinker and a formidable opponent due to his intelligence and speed-related powers.
  • Enchanced Healing
    Thawne's super-speed also grants him accelerated healing abilities, allowing him to recover from injuries faster than normal humans.
  • Discovery of Barry Allen's Identity:
    Eobard Thawne, a fanatical admirer of the Flash (Barry Allen), discovered the Flash's secret identity, which had been hidden for years. This revelation fueled his obsession with Barry Allen and set him on a path to become the Reverse-Flash.
  • Time Travel Experiments:
    Thawne's experiments with time travel led to his transformation into the Reverse-Flash. His ill-fated attempt to recreate the accident that gave Barry Allen his powers resulted in his own disfigurement and madness.
  • Becoming Barry Allen's Archenemy:
    After gaining his powers and adopting the name Reverse-Flash, Eobard Thawne became one of Barry Allen's most dangerous foes. His relentless pursuit of the Flash and his desire to prove himself as the superior speedster led to numerous confrontations.
  • Crisis on Infinite Earths:
    Eobard Thawne played a significant role in the iconic DC Comics event, Crisis on Infinite Earths. During this crossover event, he attempted to manipulate the timeline to his advantage, which had far-reaching consequences for the DC Multiverse.
  • Identity Crisis:
    In the Identity Crisis storyline, Eobard Thawne played a pivotal role as a member of the Secret Society of Super Villains. He was involved in a plot to manipulate the minds of various heroes, contributing to the overarching conflict of the story.
  • Murder of Barry Allen's Mother:
    Thawne's obsession with Barry Allen led him to travel back in time and murder Barry's mother, Nora Allen. This traumatic event had a profound impact on Barry's life and was a catalyst for his decision to become the Flash.
  • Manipulating Time and Reality:
    Thawne's extensive time manipulation and reality-altering actions have caused chaos in the DC Universe. He has disrupted the timeline multiple times, creating alternate realities and paradoxes that have had far-reaching consequences.
  • Killing Other Speedsters:
    Thawne has targeted not only Barry Allen but also other speedsters, including Jay Garrick and Wally West. He has attempted to erase them from existence or manipulate them to further his own goals.
  • Manipulation of Heroes and Villains:
    Thawne is known for his ability to manipulate heroes and villains alike, pitting them against each other and exploiting their weaknesses. He has orchestrated complex schemes that have led to disastrous consequences for many characters in the DC Universe.
